Our contests are based on real-time point spreads that are used to

Instead of picking players based on their statistics, our contests are based on outcomes of games in

OVERVIEW OF GAMEPLAY

  1. Pick Your Contest

    CoverSharks is different because it lets you choose your contest and how you want to play. Enter the
    Shark Tank and choose college teams to play against the point spread or the total in our weekly games.

  2. Choose How You Want to Play

    Do you want to compete alongside your favorite teams? Do you follow the entire nation or a specific con-
    ference or region of the country? CoverSharks gives you the option to customize your play with our
    unique format of games that suits your personal preferences.

    3. SCORE POINTS AND WIN

    Once the games begin, teams and players
    compete to win points. With each win, you
    accumulate points that can be redeemed.

  3. Play for Free

    CoverSharks lets you play for free in any of our games. Want to play with more on the line than picking
    the winner? Choose from a variety of games and choose your competition each and every week of the
    season.

  4. Play with Friends, Family, Rivals & Co-workers

    Once you see for yourself how much fun you will have, play with everyone from friends to enemies for
    the ultimate game experience. Play head-to-head, in an open league or create your own custom league
    to see who has bragging rights in real time with every game.
